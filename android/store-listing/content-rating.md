# CircleOne Keyboard -- Content Rating Declaration

## Rating: Everyone

CircleOne is a utility keyboard application suitable for all ages.

## Content Rating Questionnaire Responses

The following answers apply to the Google Play content rating questionnaire
(IARC).

### Violence
- Does the app contain violence or references to violence? **No**

### Sexual Content
- Does the app contain sexual content or nudity? **No**

### Language
- Does the app contain profanity or crude humour? **No**
- Note: The keyboard allows users to type any word, but the app itself does not
  generate, suggest, or display profane content.

### Controlled Substances
- Does the app reference or depict tobacco, alcohol, or drugs? **No**

### Gambling
- Does the app contain simulated or real gambling? **No**

### User-Generated Content
- Does the app allow users to communicate or share content with others? **No**
- The keyboard is an input method only. It does not host, display, or transmit
  user-generated content.

### Personal Information
- Does the app collect personal data or device identifiers? **No**

### Advertisements
- Does the app contain advertising? **No**

### In-App Purchases
- Does the app offer in-app purchases? **No**

## Classification

Based on the above, CircleOne qualifies for:

| System | Rating |
|--------|--------|
| IARC | 3+ |
| ESRB | Everyone |
| PEGI | 3 |
| USK | 0 |
| ClassInd | L (Livre) |
| GRAC | All |

## Notes

CircleOne is a keyboard replacement app. It provides text input functionality
and does not produce, curate, or moderate any content. The word prediction
feature uses locally stored dictionaries and does not access the internet.
